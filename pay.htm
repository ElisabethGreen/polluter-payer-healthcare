Instant Living Wage Payment App

Start getting paid for your green work. Here is an instant paycheck for those who are too busy doing the work to have time to apply for the job.




Note: Beta testers will get actual payment in the currency made available by investors and donors. Currently, payment is in the form of carbon tokens. Verified work is compensated through carbon credits if you are lifetime climate-neutral or offsets if you are not. If you do not receive payment immediately, it could be because your account does not show your comprehensive lifetime environmental impact. Use this comprehensive lifetime environmental impact calculator that you may help construct at the github repository to calculate your comprehensive lifetime environmental impact. The results will be linked to your wallet address, or if you have none, to your street address. Reducing your lifetime environmental impact will accelerate the delivery of your payment.
